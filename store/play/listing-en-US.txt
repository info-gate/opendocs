### Full description — 4000 char limit / 0 hashtags
OpenDocs is a lightweight, free document viewer for all standard file formats.
No sign-up, no subscription, no in-app purchases. Just open and read.

[Supported formats]
PDF · HWP (Korean Hangul) · DOCX (Word) · XLSX (Excel) · PPTX (PowerPoint) · EPUB · TXT · Images (JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF)

[Key features]
• Fast launch — opens instantly without heavy office suites
• Clean viewer — page swipe, pinch zoom, dark mode
• Recent files manager — quick access to documents you read often
• Favorites — pin important files to the top
• Privacy-first — all processing happens on your device, nothing uploaded

[Why OpenDocs?]
- Completely free, supported by ads (no ads inside the document viewer)
- No account required, minimal permissions
- Native HWP support for Korean documents
- Lightweight and fast (works on older devices)
- Multilingual UI — English, Korean, Japanese

[Who is it for?]
• Professionals reviewing PDFs and Word docs on the go
• Students studying class materials on mobile
• Korean users who frequently open HWP files
• Anyone who wants a lightweight viewer instead of a heavy office app

OpenDocs is built to save you time.
Install now and open any document, anywhere.
